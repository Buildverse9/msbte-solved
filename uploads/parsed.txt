 PRACTICAL NO. 08: DESCRIBE ANY THREE TECHNICAL OBJECTS USING CORRECT GRAMMAR



Conclusion and Recommendation:

Technical object description trains engineers to detail components, materials, and mechanisms objectively. It is recommended to use accurate descriptive technical adjectives and passive voice structures.



Practical Related Questions:

 Describe technical objects. (ANY THREE)



1. USB Flash Drive: A USB flash drive is a portable, solid-state electronic data storage device used to save and transfer computer files. It consists of a small printed circuit board housed inside a durable plastic or metal casing. It operates on flash memory protocols and features an integrated USB Type-A or Type-C connector plug. It is highly valued for its lightweight structure, high read-write speeds, and ease of use across computer devices.



2. Digital Multimeter: A digital multimeter is an electronic measuring instrument used by technical professionals to check electrical parameters such as voltage, resistance, and current. It features a clear central LCD digital screen, a rotatable function selector dial, and two insulated test probes (red and black). It converts analog electrical signals into digital readings, providing safe and precise troubleshooting observations for circuit boards.



3. Optical Mouse: An optical mouse is a hand-held computer input pointing device that detects two-dimensional motion relative to a flat surface. Its essential parts include an internal light-emitting diode (LED), an optical sensor mechanism, a clickable scroll wheel, and dual click buttons. It functions by projecting light onto the desk surface, capturing thousands of images per second to guide the cursor coordinates smoothly on the computer screen.
